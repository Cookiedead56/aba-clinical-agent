### Problem Behavior History

**FBA Analysis Details**: [[Client-Demo-Alex - FBA Report]]

#### 1. Social Avoidance Behavior (Primary Target)
- **Operational Definition**: During group activities or peer interaction situations, Alex leaves his current position (stands up and walks/runs at least 2 meters away), disengaging from ongoing social interaction
- **Function Hypothesis**: **Escape** - escaping high-demand social interaction requirements (social negative reinforcement)
- **Baseline Frequency**: Approximately 4-5 times/30 minutes during group activities
- **Intervention Strategy**: Antecedent manipulation (reduce social demand gradient) + teach functional replacement behavior "I need a break" + differential reinforcement (DRA)

#### 2. Vocalization During Group Activities
- **Operational Definition**: During group activities, producing high-pitched vocalizations unrelated to the current activity (>2 seconds)
- **Function Hypothesis**: **Attention seeking** (possibly with automatic reinforcement component)
- **Baseline Frequency**: Approximately 2-3 times per group activity
- **Intervention Strategy**: Non-contingent reinforcement (NCR) + teach appropriate attention-getting replacement (tapping teacher's arm) + planned ignoring

#### 3. Task Avoidance (Pushing Materials Away)
- **Operational Definition**: During tabletop instruction, using hands to push teaching materials away or push them off the table
- **Function Hypothesis**: **Escape** - escaping non-preferred or high-demand academic tasks
- **Baseline Frequency**: Approximately 3 times/20 minutes during non-preferred tasks
- **Intervention Strategy**: High-probability request sequence (high-p) + task interspersal (alternating preferred/non-preferred) + teach "help me" replacement communication
